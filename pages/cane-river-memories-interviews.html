Cane River Memories Interviews

As part of this past project for the Cane River National Heritage Area I converted some old oral history interviews to mp3. They are real treasures, and there are many more out there. They were originally recorded as part of the Retired Senior Volunteer Project, and are currently housed in the Cammie G. Henry Research Library. I'm just stashing a few here for now.
